About Me

❮ ❯

Hi, I see you found my Webpage!

So let me tell a bit about myself

I'm a 17 year old teenager currently living and studying in Switzerland. Currently I'm focusing on studying math and physics, although I'd like to start an education in IT and cyber security.

In my free time, I'm greatly interested in the field of technology and computers as a whole. I have some experience in programming with different languages such as Python, JavaScript, SQL and PHP (yes, PHP. it's not completely useless) and some basic knowledge in Java.

In order to get some fresh air, I enjoy sailing and I'm part of a table tennis club in my region, as well as being in a leading role in the local scouts. Finally, I'm also active in action sports such mountain biking and freeride skiing.
